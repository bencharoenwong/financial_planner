Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

The Short Version

  • ✓ Your financial data stays on your device
  • ✓ We don't store your inputs or results on our servers
  • ✓ We don't sell or share any data with third parties
  • ✓ No account required to use the calculator
  • ✓ We only collect what's needed for payments (if you purchase reports)

1. What We Don't Collect

Unlike most financial tools, we take a privacy-first approach. We do not collect or store:

  • • Your financial inputs (current savings, income, goals)
  • • Your calculation results or success probabilities
  • • Bank account information or transaction data
  • • Investment account details or balances
  • • Social Security numbers or government IDs
  • • Your name, address, or phone number (unless you contact us)

2. How the Calculator Works

When you use our calculator:

  1. 1. You enter your information
    Your data stays in your browser's memory
  2. 2. Calculation happens
    Your inputs are sent to our API for Monte Carlo simulation. The API processes the request and returns results immediately—we don't log or store the inputs.
  3. 3. Results displayed
    Results are shown in your browser. Nothing is stored on our servers.
  4. 4. You close the page
    Your data is gone (unless you saved scenarios to your browser's local storage, which stays on your device).

3. What We Do Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for operations:

Anonymous Usage Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (like Plausible or self-hosted solutions) to understand how people use the tool. This includes page views and general usage patterns, but no personal identifiers.

Payment Information (If You Purchase)

If you purchase a report, we use Stripe to process payments. Stripe collects:

  • • Email address (for receipt delivery)
  • • Payment method (handled entirely by Stripe)
  • • Transaction record (for refunds and support)

We never see or store your full credit card number. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Support Communications

If you email us, we'll keep that correspondence to help resolve your issue.

4. Local Storage

If you save scenarios, they're stored in your browser's local storage:

  • • This data never leaves your device
  • • We cannot access it
  • • You can clear it anytime via your browser settings
  • • It persists until you clear your browser data

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • • Essential cookies: For basic site functionality (e.g., remembering your preferences)
  • • No tracking cookies: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking tools
  • • No advertising cookies: We don't run ads

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • • Stripe: Payment processing (privacy policy)
  • • Vercel: Website hosting (privacy policy)

7. Data Retention

  • • Calculator inputs/outputs: Not retained (processed in memory only)
  • • Payment records: Kept for 7 years (legal/tax requirements)
  • • Support emails: Kept for 2 years unless you request deletion
  • • Local storage: Controlled by you in your browser

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • • Request a copy of any data we have about you
  • • Request deletion of your data
  • • Opt out of any future communications
  • • Clear your local storage at any time

Contact us at privacy@chicagoglobal.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll note the "last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
